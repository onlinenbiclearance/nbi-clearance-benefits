NBI Clearance Benefits & Who Can Apply

A complete and up-to-date guide to understanding the importance, uses, and eligibility for NBI Clearance in the Philippines.

📅 Updated 2026 ⏱ 5 min read 🇵🇭 For Filipinos

In the Philippines, the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) Clearance is one of the most important government documents a person can have. Whether you are applying for a job, traveling abroad, or processing a legal matter, this document plays a critical role in verifying your background and establishing your good standing as a citizen.

In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know — from the key benefits of obtaining NBI Clearance to the different types of individuals who can apply for it.

💡 Quick Note: NBI Clearance is issued by the National Bureau of Investigation and certifies that the bearer has no pending criminal case or derogatory record on file.

What Is NBI Clearance?

NBI Clearance is an official document issued by the National Bureau of Investigation of the Philippines. It serves as proof that the bearer does not have any pending criminal cases or derogatory records in the NBI database. It is widely accepted as a standard background verification document across various sectors — from private companies to government agencies.

Key Benefits of NBI Clearance

The NBI Clearance offers a wide range of benefits that make it an essential document for every Filipino. Here are the most important ones:

💼

Employment Requirement

Most companies in the Philippines require NBI Clearance before hiring. It assures employers that a candidate has a clean background.

✈️

Overseas Work & Travel

Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) need NBI Clearance when applying for work abroad or processing visa applications.

⚖️

Legal Transactions

Required for court filings, legal proceedings, and government transactions that need identity and background verification.

🏫

School & Scholarship Applications

Some universities and scholarship programs request NBI Clearance as part of the application requirements.

🏛️

Government Licensing

Required for professional license applications with PRC, business permits, and other government-regulated activities.

🔒

Personal Background Verification

Individuals can use it to verify their own records and ensure their personal data in the NBI system is accurate and clean.

Who Can Apply for NBI Clearance?

One of the most common questions people ask is: who is eligible to get an NBI Clearance? The good news is that the eligibility is quite broad and covers almost all individuals with ties to the Philippines.

  • Filipino Citizens – Any Filipino national, whether residing locally or abroad, is eligible to apply for NBI Clearance.
  • Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) – OFWs often need clearance before deploying abroad or renewing their work contracts overseas.
  • Dual Citizens – Individuals holding dual Filipino citizenship can apply using their Philippine passport or valid government ID.
  • Foreign Nationals Residing in the Philippines – Foreigners with valid residency in the Philippines may also be required to obtain NBI Clearance for employment or legal purposes.
  • Students & Fresh Graduates – Those entering the job market for the first time are commonly asked to present NBI Clearance during job applications.
  • Professionals & Licensed Workers – Doctors, engineers, nurses, and other licensed professionals often need clearance for licensing renewals with the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC).
  • Business Owners & Entrepreneurs – Those applying for business permits or engaging in government contracts may also be required to submit clearance.

How Long Is NBI Clearance Valid?

NBI Clearance is typically valid for one (1) year from the date of issuance. However, validity can vary depending on the purpose. Some employers and agencies may accept a clearance that is only a few months old, while others require a freshly issued one. Always check with the requesting institution to confirm their requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can minors apply for NBI Clearance?

Generally, NBI Clearance is for individuals who are 18 years old and above. Minors may apply in special cases with parental consent, but this is not a standard requirement.

Is NBI Clearance required for all jobs?

Not all jobs require it, but the vast majority of employers — especially in the corporate, government, and healthcare sectors — include NBI Clearance as part of the pre-employment requirements.

Can I apply for NBI Clearance online?

Yes, the NBI now offers an online application system that allows applicants to register, schedule appointments, and pay fees digitally — making the process much more convenient.

What if I have a "HIT" on my NBI record?

A "HIT" means the system found a record matching your name. This does not automatically mean you have a criminal case — it could be a name match. You will be required to appear in person for verification and clearance of the record.

Why NBI Clearance Matters

Beyond its practical uses, NBI Clearance serves as a foundation of trust in Philippine society. It helps employers make informed hiring decisions, protects communities by screening individuals in sensitive roles, and gives Filipinos a credible document to present in both local and international settings.

For OFWs especially, having a valid and updated NBI Clearance is not just a formality — it is often a non-negotiable requirement that opens doors to better employment opportunities abroad. It reassures foreign employers and agencies that Filipino workers come with verified and trustworthy backgrounds.

Final Thoughts

NBI Clearance is more than just a piece of paper — it is your passport to employment, legal credibility, and peace of mind. Whether you are a fresh graduate, a seasoned professional, or an OFW preparing to work abroad, keeping your NBI Clearance updated is a smart and responsible step every Filipino should take.
